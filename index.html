04 — Selected Projects

01

SME Credit Readiness Dashboard

Data Analysis UMKM Lending Looker Studio
Problem

Business banking teams often face difficulty assessing UMKM credit readiness because data is fragmented and informal. A simple readiness dashboard can support faster, more consistent first-pass screening.

Approach

Synthesized publicly available BPS and OJK data into a five-indicator readiness framework covering revenue stability, digital adoption, bookkeeping discipline, growth trend, and business permit status. Modeled twenty hypothetical UMKM profiles based on aggregate sector characteristics.

Relevance to BTN Business Banking

Directly applicable to KUR-style screening and pre-qualification workflows. Demonstrates credit screening intuition without requiring access to confidential customer data — a starting framework that can be refined with real underwriting policies.

Primary Research Customer Segmentation UMKM

Relevance to BTN Business Banking
